acknowledgment of our privilege

draft by claude wittmann,on July 8, 2021




as artists, we acknowledge the privilege to be seen as white and/or educated and/or cismale and thus to be treated with a certain level of priority to the basic right to housing.

we acknowledge that we are currently housed while still some of us and a lot of others in Toronto are going through grueling housing insecurity.

we acknowledge that most if not all of us have not been a resident of an encampment and thus not experienced the City of Toronto's violent evictions.
